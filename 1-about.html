about us

Macedonian Esports Federation is a nonprofit organization responsible for esports competitions and tournaments. Our mission is promotion of esports and recognition from the government institutions. MESF organizes national esports league and qualification tournaments for the World and European esports championships.
The Federation currently has 7 permanent clubs as founding members and more than 120 teams participating in tournaments. Officially registered in 2009, we are the oldest esports organization in the region.
We are part of the international competitions since 2005. Until now we have taken part in 11 international championships, bearing the flag of Macedonia. MESF is a member of IESF (International Esports Federation) and WESCO (World Esports Consortium) and since 2020, one of the founders of the European Esports Federation.
